Communications Lab Fall 2024
Memory Soundscape

Artist: Yawen Li

In the dream, the protagonist gets distracted while driving, leading to a car accident, and ultimately wakes up in a state of tension and fear to the sound of an alarm clock. The work aims to provide the audience with an immersive dreaming experience. While composing the audio, the creator tries to capture her personal experience of being jolted awake by an alarm clock in a dream.

Memory Soundscape is a collection of audio works. Use the left and right arrow keys to switch between them, and wear headphones to listen.
